USM in a nutshell

What is USM – and why does it feel different?

Unified Service Management (USM) is an enterprise service management architecture. It gives you one simple way to design, run, and improve services – whether you manage applications, buildings, HR, or a shared service center.

  • Replaces dozens of overlapping “best practices” with one coherent method
  • Works with ITIL, COBIT, ISO, Lean, Agile, DevOps – it doesn’t fight them
  • Uses a small, visual language (5 processes, 8 workflows) that everyone can learn
  • Supports governance, sourcing, and shared service centers out of the box

As your coach, I help you:

  • Translate the book and theory into your real-world environment
  • Align leaders, teams, and tools around a single management system
  • Build routines that survive reorganizations, new tools, and new frameworks
